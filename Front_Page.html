About the Sahasam Game

The Ultimate Treasure Hunt Adventure!

Get ready to experience Sahasam, a thrilling treasure hunt game inspired by the blockbuster movie! In this exciting adventure, you will sign up as an explorer, create your own profile, and start a journey to uncover hidden treasures worth up to ₹1 lakh. The game begins by collecting 6 special keys from famous characters of the Sahasam movie, who will guide you through fun and challenging mini-games. Each mini-game brings you closer to the final treasure while you earn real rewards along the way. After gathering all the keys, you get to spin a prize wheel to instantly win up to ₹20.

But the excitement doesn't stop there—brace yourself for the ultimate underground maze challenge, where hidden treasures and big cash prizes await. You can even book your movie ticket, track your rewards, and invite friends using your referral ID to earn more. Whether you love solving puzzles, exploring stories, or just want to win amazing prizes, Sahasam offers an adventure like no other. Are you ready to join the hunt?

How to Play

🗝️

1 Registration

Embark on your journey by creating your adventurer profile. Enter your full name, choose an adventurous username, and set your secret code to protect your treasures. Verify your phone number with the OTP sent via SMS, and unlock the gateway to a world of hidden riches and thrilling challenges.

🧭

2 Exploration

Step into your personal dashboard, the command center for your expedition. Here you can book tickets to watch the epic Sahasam movie, monitor your growing collection of rewards, and share your unique referral ID with fellow explorers to earn bonus treasures. Every action you take brings you closer to unlocking the grand prize.

🧩

3 Key Collection

Prepare to meet legendary characters from the Sahasam movie who will be your guides and challengers. Engage with Aju Varghese, Althaf Salim, Gouri G. Kishan, and others as you test your skills in six captivating mini-games. Each victory earns you a powerful key, bringing you one step closer to unlocking the ultimate treasure chamber.

💰

4 Final Challenge

With all six keys in hand, it’s time to spin the wheel of fortune for instant rewards up to ₹20. But the true test awaits underground—navigate a mysterious maze filled with hidden treasures and secret passages. Conquer every obstacle to claim cash prizes ranging from ₹10,000 to ₹1 lakh and etch your name among the greatest treasure hunters.

Game Rules & Regulations

  • Players must register with a valid phone number and verify it using the OTP sent via SMS.
  • Each player must create a unique adventurer profile and keep their secret code confidential at all times.
  • Only one account per player is permitted throughout the entire treasure hunt.
  • All six keys must be collected by successfully completing the mini-games hosted by the Sahasam movie characters.
  • Sharing answers, solutions, or walkthroughs publicly is strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.
  • After collecting all keys, players must spin the prize wheel before entering the underground maze challenge.
  • Cheating, using unauthorized tools, or exploiting glitches will result in immediate suspension of the account.
  • Rewards earned through gameplay and referrals will be credited to your profile wallet according to the terms.
  • Each player has only one chance per day to attempt the underground maze challenge.Choose your time wisely!
  • All decisions made by the game moderators are final and binding.
