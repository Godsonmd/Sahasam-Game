Sahasam Official Game Guide

About the Game

Sahasam is a thrilling treasure hunt adventure inspired by the Sahasam movie. Players embark on an exciting quest to discover hidden treasures worth up to ₹1 lakh, meet beloved film characters, solve mini-games, and conquer an underground maze challenge.

The game combines strategy, puzzle-solving, and luck in an immersive experience that brings your "sahasam" to life.

Getting Started

  • Visit the home page to explore game details and movie cast information.
  • Control the home screen audio using the enable/disable button.
  • Click the "Are You Ready" button to proceed to sign up.
  • Enter your full name, phone number, optional referral ID, and Secret Code password.
  • Confirm your Secret Code by retyping it, then click "Join the Hunt."
  • Receive an OTP via SMS and verify it to activate your account.
  • If you already have an account, use the Already have an account option to log in.

Creating Your Profile

After login, you are taken to your explorer profile. From this dashboard, you can:

  • Book movie tickets for Sahasam.
  • Share your referral ID with friends to earn bonus rewards.
  • Track all rewards and prizes earned during gameplay.
  • Read the comic story introducing your treasure hunt journey.
  • View your progress in collecting the 6 magical keys.

How the Game Works

Your main mission is to find 6 magical keys by completing challenges with NPC characters. Each NPC is a beloved movie character who provides clues, rules, and guidance for mini-games.

  • When you start the game, you need to complete a task and watch the teaser to earn 10 coins.
  • Meet Aju Varghese, Althaf Salim, Jayasree Sivadas, Narain, Baiju Santhosh, Gouri G. Kishan, and Babu Antony.
  • Each character hosts a unique mini-game you must complete to earn a key.
  • For every 2 keys you find, you can spin the prize wheel to instantly win up to 20 coins.
  • Finally, tackle the underground maze challenge to uncover the secret code and win cash prizes up to ₹1 lakh.
  • Discover special bonus easter eggs hidden throughout the game environment.

Rules & Regulations

  • Players must be at least 13 years old.
  • Only one account per player is allowed.
  • Sharing solutions or cheat codes is strictly prohibited.
  • Cheating or exploiting the game will lead to disqualification.
  • Each player gets one chance daily to play the underground maze.
  • All decisions by game moderators are final.
  • Prizes are subject to verification and may take 25-35 business days to process.
