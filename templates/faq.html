Sahasam FAQs

How do I register for Sahasam?

Click the "Are You Ready" button on the homepage. Enter your full name, phone number, optional referral ID, and create your Secret Code password. An OTP will be sent via SMS to verify your account.

What rewards can I win?

You can earn coins which can be converted into real cash. The amount will be sent to your bank account via UPI ID or bank account details within 30 days after the event ends. If you win the main prize, after August 8 it will be sent to you accordingly.

How many times can I play the maze?

Each player gets one chance daily to play the underground maze. Plan your moves carefully! Your progress is saved automatically, so you can continue your adventure the next day.

Can I share my solutions?

No. Sharing solutions or cheat codes publicly is strictly prohibited and may result in disqualification. We want to keep the adventure fair and exciting for all treasure hunters!

Who are the NPC characters?

They are inspired by the Sahasam movie cast: Aju Varghese, Althaf Salim, Jayasree Sivadas, Narain, Baiju Santhosh, Gouri G. Kishan, and Babu Antony. Each guides you through unique challenges to collect 6 keys that unlock the final treasure chamber.

How do referrals work?

Share your referral ID with friends. For every friend who joins using your code, you get extra chances to guess and win the final award.

What should I do if I forget my Secret Code?

Use the "Forgot Secret Code" option on the login page. You will receive an OTP to reset your password securely. For additional security, we recommend choosing a memorable but unique Secret Code.
