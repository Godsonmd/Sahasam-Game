Sahasam Treasure Hunt - Privacy & Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Sahasam, an adventure treasure hunt inspired by the Sahasam movie. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information while you enjoy the thrill of finding hidden treasures, solving mini-games, and exploring the underground maze.

Information We Collect

During registration and gameplay, we collect certain details to create your adventurer profile and enhance your experience:

  • Full Name(for account verification)
  • Phone Number (for account verification and OTPs)
  • Optional Referral ID (to track rewards)
  • Password (your Secret Code, stored securely)
  • Gameplay data such as scores, achievements, and rewards earned

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Send OTP verification codes via SMS
  • Allow you to participate in challenges, spin the reward wheel, and navigate the underground maze
  • Track your progress and display rewards, referrals, and leaderboard status
  • Improve the game experience and provide support when needed

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • With trusted service providers (e.g., SMS gateways) necessary to deliver OTPs and communications

Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your Secret Code (password). Do not share your login credentials or solutions to game challenges publicly. Sharing solutions is strictly prohibited.

Age Requirements

Players must be at least 13 years old to participate in Sahasam.

Daily Play and Fair Use

Each player is allowed only one chance per day to guess the underground maze challenge. Cheating, using exploits, or maintaining multiple accounts may result in suspension or permanent disqualification.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy & Policy page to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this page regularly.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact our support team at support@sahasamgame.com.
